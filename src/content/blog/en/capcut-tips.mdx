## Why CapCut?

CapCut is the most powerful free video editor available today, especially when combined with FlashCut.ai automation. Here are 10 tips to optimize your workflow.

## Tip 1: Use Keyframe Animations

FlashCut automatically creates keyframe animations for images. You can fine-tune them in CapCut:

- **Zoom In/Out** — create Ken Burns effect for static images
- **Pan & Tilt** — smooth camera movements
- **Scale & Rotate** — combine multiple effects

## Tip 2: Leverage Auto Subtitles

FlashCut integrates Deepgram STT with 95.5% accuracy. Subtitles are automatically synced with the timeline.

## Tip 3: Effects & Transitions

Use the optimized effects collection:

- **Opening Effects** — impressive intro animations
- **Smooth Transitions** — seamless scene changes
- **Color Grading** — professional color filters

## Tip 4: Track Layering

FlashCut's Tiered Track Layering system ensures:

- Video tracks are always on the correct layer
- Text overlays always display on top
- Audio tracks are mixed precisely

## Tip 5: Export Settings

Optimal export settings for YouTube:

- **Resolution**: 1080p or 4K
- **FPS**: 30 or 60
- **Bitrate**: High quality for detailed content

## Conclusion

Combining FlashCut.ai with CapCut helps you create professional videos faster than ever. Apply these tips to elevate your video quality!
