## Introduction

FlashCut.ai is an AI automation platform that helps you create professional YouTube videos in just minutes. Instead of spending hours on manual editing, FlashCut automates the entire workflow from script to finished video.

## Step 1: Prepare Your Script

Start by inputting your script into FlashCut. The system supports multiple formats:

- **JSON Pipeline** — standard format with image prompts and voice-over
- **Plain text** — AI will automatically analyze and create segments
- **File import** — supports .txt, .json, .md

## Step 2: Let AI Do the Work

FlashCut leverages over 10 leading AI tools:

- **Gemini & Claude** for content analysis and optimization
- **Midjourney & Flux** for high-quality image generation
- **ElevenLabs & Deepgram** for natural voice-over

## Step 3: Export to CapCut

Once AI finishes processing, FlashCut creates a complete CapCut project with:

- Synchronized timeline
- Professional effects and transitions
- Automatic subtitles
- Matching background music

## Conclusion

With FlashCut.ai, you can create studio-quality videos without any video editing experience. Try it today!
