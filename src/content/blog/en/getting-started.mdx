## Introduction

FlashCut.ai is an AI automation platform that helps you create professional YouTube videos in just minutes. Instead of spending hours manually editing, FlashCut automates the entire pipeline from script to finished video.

## Step 1: Prepare Your Script

Start by inputting your script into FlashCut. The system supports multiple formats:

- **JSON Pipeline** — standard format with image prompts and voice-over
- **Plain text** — AI will automatically analyze and create segments
- **Import from file** — supports .txt, .json, .md

![Script input interface](/blog/script-input.png)

## Step 2: Let AI Handle It

FlashCut uses over 10 leading AI tools:

- **Gemini & Claude** for content analysis and optimization
- **Midjourney & Flux** for high-quality image generation
- **ElevenLabs & Deepgram** for natural voice-over

![AI processing pipeline](/blog/ai-processing.png)

## Step 3: Export to CapCut

After AI completes processing, FlashCut creates a complete CapCut project with:

- Synced timeline
- Professional effects and transitions
- Auto-generated subtitles
- Matching background music

![CapCut timeline export](/blog/capcut-export.png)

## Conclusion

With FlashCut.ai, you can create studio-quality videos without any video editing experience. Try it today!
